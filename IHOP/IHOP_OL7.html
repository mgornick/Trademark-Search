IHOP Coupons

Categories: Food & Drink | Dining Out | Restaurants | Restaurant Coupons | Breakfast

  • IHOP Facts

    • Based in: Glendale, CA
    • Started in: 1958
    • Started by: Al Lapin, Jerry Lapin, Albert Kallis
    • Industry: Restaurant
    • Cuisine: Breakfast, pancakes, waffles, salads, lunch, dinner
    • Both franchised and company owned http://www.ihop.com/index.php?option=com...
    • Owned by: DineEquity, Inc. http://www.ihop.com/index.php?option=com...
    • Number of stores: 1433, as of September 2009 http://www.ihop.com/index.php?option=com...
    • 99% of the restaurants are independently owned and operated franchises http://www.ihop.com/index.php?option=com...

  • IHOP, known as International House of Pancakes, offers breakfast, lunch and dinner at locations all over the nation. Breakfast and pancakes (particularly fruit pancakes) are their specialty. Some IHOPs are open 24 hours a day, and others are open late-night and early-morning (contact your nearest IHOP for exact hours). This page of IHOP coupons will direct you to coupons you can use when dining out at IHOP restaurants.

  • Where to Get IHOP Coupons

    According to the IHOP official site, their most common method of coupon delivery is "the glossy coupon inserts in the Sunday newspaper."http://www.ihop.com/index.php?Itemid=5&id=21&option=com_content&task=view These inserts are produced by companies such as CoolSavings, Valassis, SmartSource, and RedPlum, who frequently post online versions of the coupons they print with Sunday supplements on their websites.


    Sometimes, coupons can also be found on various third party websites on the Internet, such as Coupons.com and Retail me Not. .

  • IHOP Coupon Policy

    IHOP regularly distributes coupons good for discounts on meals for dining in as well as take-out. These can sometimes be found on third-party coupon websites such as Coupon Craze and Dream Cuizine. The coupons found online are printable from home, and may be redeemed at participating restaurants. There are usually expiration dates, so be sure to read all information on the coupon before attempting to use it. The official IHOP website often lists the latest savings opportunities, and printable coupons may often be found on the site, as well.


    As most locations are independently owned and operated, the decision whether or not to participate in a corporate promotion is up to the manager.
