  • IHOP Facts

    • Founded in 1958 http://www.ihop.com/index.php?option=com...
    • First IHOP: Toluca Lake, California http://www.ihop.com/index.php?option=com...
    • Founders: Jerry Lapin, Al Lapin Jr., Albert Kallis, and Sherwood Rosenberg http://www.ihop.com/index.php?option=com...
    • Originally known as the International House of Pancakes http://www.ihop.com/index.php?option=com...
    • Headquarters: Glendale, California http://www.ihop.com/index.php?option=com...
    • 1960: Started franchising http://www.ihop.com/index.php?option=com...
    • 1961: First becomes publicly traded http://www.ihop.com/index.php?option=com...
    • 1973: Starts using IHOP acronym http://www.ihop.com/index.php?option=com...
    • 1984: Rooty Tooty Fresh 'n Fruity introduced http://www.ihop.com/index.php?option=com...
    • July 2007: Bought Applebee's http://consumerist.com/2007/07/ihop-buys...
    • Slogan: "Come hungry. Leave happy." http://www.ihop.com/index.php?option=com...
    • Many locations open 24 hours
    • Serves free pancakes yearly on National Pancake Day
    • National Pancake Day, also known as Fat Tuesday, falls on a Tuesday in February or March

  • IHOP, which stands for International House of Pancakes, is a restaurant chain that specializes in breakfast foods, especially pancakes. IHOP also offers menu items for lunch and dinner, plus dessert in addition to its extensive breakfast menu. IHOP locations are recognizable for their blue roofs. In 2007, IHOP bought the restaurant chain Applebee's for 2.2 billion dollars.http://consumerist.com/2007/07/ihop-buys-applebees-for-22-billion.html


    The IHOP chain of restaurants has about 1,461 stores in all 50 U.S. states, and several locations in Canada, Mexico, and Puerto Rico. The first IHOP was opened near Los Angeles, California in 1958. IHOP's current headquarters are in Glendale, California.http://www.ihop.com/index.php?option=com_content&task=view&id=20&Itemid=5


    IHOP also offers free pancakes to its customers at participating stores on National Pancake Day, which is celebrated on Shrove Tuesday, also known as Fat Tuesday the day before Lent begins.http://www.ihoppancakeday.com/pancake-day-details.html

  • IHOP Menu

    IHOP offers menu options for breakfast, lunch and dinner. Classic American breakfasts such as pancakes, omelets, eggs made to customer specs, French Toast, hash browns and an assortment of breakfast meats. Internationally inspired crepes are also available. For lunch, guests can order sandwiches, burgers and salads, and dinner items include traditional fare such as pot roast, steak tips and chicken strips.http://www.ihop.com/index.php?option=com_content&task=view&id=31&Itemid=2
